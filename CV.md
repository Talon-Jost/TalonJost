## 🧬 Research Experience

**Eastern Washington University** — 2024  
*Project:* Understanding the Role of Tetrodotoxin on Skin Microbiome Composition and Chytrid Fungal Infection in Rough-skinned Newts (*Taricha granulosa*)  
- Investigated how an endogenously produced neurotoxin regulates *Batrachochytrium dendrobatidis* infection and amphibian skin microbiome composition.  
- Conducted extensive statistical and bioinformatic analyses of microbial communities.  
- Employed PCR, DNA extraction, and isolate culturing to characterize host–pathogen–microbiome–tetrodotoxin interactions.  

**Eastern Washington University** — 2023  
*Project:* Evaluating Efficacy of Anti-*Batrachochytrium dendrobatidis* Probiotic Treatment on Pacific Chorus Frogs (*Pseudacris regilla*) at Current and Modeled Climate Change Temperatures  
- Collected *P. regilla* from Turnbull National Wildlife Refuge for controlled isolation experiments.  
- Assessed microbiome resilience and probiotic efficacy under current and future temperature regimes.  
- Applied aseptic technique and a range of field, laboratory, and microbial methods with animal care considerations.  

**University of Arkansas – Fayetteville** — 2022  
*Project:* Western Chicken Turtle Habitat Occupancy Modeling  
- Continued and curated a multi-year field study in challenging wetland environments.  
- Deployed, monitored, and maintained field equipment for occupancy modeling of *Deirochelys reticulata*.  
- Conducted live capture, marking, and release, vegetation surveys, and statistical analysis of habitat use.  

**United States Department of Defense** — 2021  
*Position:* US Army Corps of Engineers Park Ranger / Naturalist  
- Enforced federal rules and hunting/fishing regulations on government property.  
- Maintained vehicles, facilities, and grounds while ensuring visitor safety.  
- Handled venomous and dangerous wildlife and coordinated responses with local and federal officials.  

**Rogers State University** — 2020  
*Project:* Semi-Arid Prairie Restoration  
- Contributed to long-term restoration of mixed-grass prairie using vegetation removal, herbicide application, and pyric herbivory.  
- Prepared habitat for reintroduction of the critically endangered American Burying Beetle (*Nicrophorus americanus*).  

**Rogers State University** — 2020  
*Project:* Herpetological Biodiversity Assessment  
- Collected and identified herpetofauna to species level in state-designated nature reserves.  
- Used pit tagging/toe clipping with sterile handling to support concurrent research projects.  

**Oklahoma Aquarium** — 2020  
*Position:* Shark and Large Animal Quarantine Department  
- Cared for quarantined large marine and freshwater species prior to exhibition.  
- Monitored health, pathology, diet, and water quality.  
- Engaged in public outreach with live-animal demonstrations and education.
